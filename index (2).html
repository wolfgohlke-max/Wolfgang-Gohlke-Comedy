THE STORY

My name is Wolfgang. My dad is German. My mom is Freddy Mercury. I have Borderline Personality Disorder, which means I wake up at 4:40 AM to go to the gym—not 4:45, because if I change my routine by five minutes, I'm convinced my entire life will collapse.

I used to be a financial planner. I made negative $3,000. Most of my clients were women I met on dating apps.

I'm moving from Wilmington, NC to Austin, TX to pursue comedy full-time because I've realized the only thing I'm actually good at is admitting how absurd everything is—including myself.

My comedy is about contradiction, vulnerability, and dark specificity. It's not about being the funniest person in the room. It's about being the most honest person in the room while pointing out life's absurdities.
